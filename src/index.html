Game rules

Break bricks, level up, and choose your build… or let me handle it.

  • Objective: destroy bricks, level up, and push your score higher.
  • Lives: you lose a life when a brick exits at the bottom.
  • Fire: manual mode lets you aim and launch; auto-aim fires for you.
  • Powers: each level offers a power or upgrade; marked balls apply it on hit.
  • Talents: passive bonuses (speed, paddle width, precision, etc.).
  • Slots: max 4 powers and 4 talents; fusions can replace ingredients to fit.
  • Fusions: some pairs at level 3 unlock a Fusion (e.g., Fire + Light → Sun) and taking it consumes the ingredients.
